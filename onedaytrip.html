'One Day' Trip


Choose your favorite from the closest places from

Padma Resort

A beautiful resort near infamous Mawa ghat, situated in the banks of Padma. It offers a beautiful place that serves as a great picnic spot.

There is nothing like tracking down the Chandranath hill in 24 thrilling hours, situated in Shitakundu, 170 km away from Dhaka.

Bongobondhu Safari park is a beautiful scenic place located in North-West of Dhaka, near Gazipur, full of green beauty.
