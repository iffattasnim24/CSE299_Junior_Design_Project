Popular Destinations

pick and choose from our popular destinations section to visit the most marvelious scenic places Bangladesh has to offer!

Sundarban

The Sundarbans is a mangrove area in the delta formed by the confluence of the Ganges, Brahmaputra and Meghna Rivers in the Bay of Bengal.

Saint martin

St. Martin is generally known as “Narikel Zinzira” in Bengali, means 'Coconut Island' and this is the only coral reef island in Bangladesh.

Sreemangal

Sreemangal is often referred to as the 'tea capital' of Bangladesh, and is most famous for its tea fields. It is situated in Sylhet division.

Bandarban

one of the three hill districts of Bangladesh and a part of the Chittagong Hill Tracts. Other 2 being Khagrachari and Rangamati.

Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is situated along the Bay of Bengal about 100km south of Chittagong. Contains the longest sea beach in the world.

Khagrachari

one of the three hill districts of Bangladesh and a part of the Chittagong Hill Tracts. Other 2 being Bandarban and Rangamati.
